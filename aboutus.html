Welcome to Chonky Cat Café!

About Us

Welcome to the Chonky Cat Café – a place where coffee meets companionship! Our café offers a unique blend of cozy coffeehouse vibes with the comforting presence of friendly felines. Whether you’re looking for a calm corner to work, relax, or enjoy your favorite brew, our cats are always here to make your experience a little brighter.

Our Mission

At Chonky Cat Café, our mission is to create a joyful environment where both humans and cats can come together to relax and unwind. We strive to create a peaceful atmosphere where our visitors can enjoy high-quality coffee, delicious snacks, and the company of our adorable resident cats – all of whom are available for adoption!

Our Vision

We believe in making the world a little more paw-sitive, one cup of coffee at a time! Our vision is to create a space that not only brings joy to visitors but also helps cats in need find loving forever homes. Every sip you take is a step towards giving a stray or abandoned cat a second chance at life.

Meet the Team

Messi - Founder

Messi love for cats and coffee inspired her to create Chonky Cat Café, a sanctuary for both animals and people. She’s always here with a smile and a purring kitty by her side!

Sir Bingus - Head Barista

Bingus brews the best cappuccino in town and loves sharing his coffee knowledge with visitors. His favorite thing? Seeing guests bond with our cats while enjoying his coffee!

Silly Goobert - Cat Caretaker

Silly ensures that our cats are happy, healthy, and ready to meet you. She’s passionate about animal welfare and is always on hand to help you find your purr-fect feline companion.
